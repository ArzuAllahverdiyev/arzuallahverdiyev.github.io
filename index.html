About

Who I am as an engineer

I enjoy taking messy, siloed data and turning it into well-structured, trustworthy datasets that help teams make better decisions.

I’m a Data Engineer with experience in banking and insurance, working across data warehousing, lakehouse patterns, and analytics engineering.

My work often involves designing ETL/ELT processes, building data models (from bronze/silver/gold layers to dimensional models), and integrating tools like Airflow, dbt, Spark, Kafka, and modern databases.

Outside of production work, I also enjoy teaching and mentoring, building learning materials and realistic data environments for people entering the data field.

What I can help with
  • • Designing end-to-end data pipelines (from source systems to BI)
  • • Building robust DWH / lakehouse architectures
  • • Modeling data for analytics and reporting (Kimball, vault-style)
  • • Implementing dbt projects, tests and documentation
  • • Optimizing SQL / Spark jobs for performance and reliability
  • • Teaching SQL, Python, and data engineering foundations
